Design Elements

Logo




Logo Description


Our company's logo features the world depicted in the shape of an onion. This symbolizes our commitment to connecting people with their traditional food, regardless of where they travel. (We believe that food is an essential part of every culture, and we want to help people retain that connection to their heritage. With our services, our customers can explore the world without sacrificing the flavors and dishes they love. We are proud to be part of a global community that values diversity and celebrates food as a unifying force.)

Testing Report

Please write about…

  • Goals:
    • The website seeks user input and critique on the aesthetics and design of its homepage.
    • The website aims to inform visitors about the company's offerings and enable them to make investments.

  • Testing methods
    • Participants: 2
    • Setting
      User 1: Remotely, Desktop
      User 2: Home, Mobile 
    • Protocol:  
      Thinking Aloud: Participants are prompted to express their feelings and observations as they use the website or carry out particular tasks. This protocol gives information about how they make decisions and aids in detecting usability problems.

      Task-based Testing: Participants complete predetermined activities on the website while their interactions and success rates are tracked. This procedure evaluates the website's efficacy and efficiency in assisting users in finishing their tasks.

  • Results:
    • User 1:

      • Positives - Likes the colour scheme 

      Negatives/improvements:
    • Busy Homepage: User 1 advises shifting the carousel to a different page or only keeping the carousel on the homepage as he describes the homepage as being too busy. This suggests that there may be too much content or distracting components on the page.

    • User 1 advises expanding the first carousel's information on the company's offerings. This criticism points out a chance to improve the company's offerings' presentation and clarity.

    •  Investment Options: User 1 suggests adding a button that would let people choose an investment amount and make an investment. This recommendation represents a constructive step toward enhancing user engagement and opening up the investing process.

    • User 2: 

      Positives: 
    • Visually appealing: The participant remarks that the website is adorable and finds the moving images to be intriguing.
    • The participant expresses enthusiasm for the carousel, calling it “really attractive”.
    • User-Friendly Process: The participant reports that the procedure for choosing a reward tier and making a financial contribution was easy to use and straightforward.

      Negatives/improvements:
    • Excessive Text: The participant complains that the website has too much text and proposes that it be cut or condensed to improve readability and user experience.

    • The participant comments that the website's values section feels a little empty, implying that it might be filled with more information or material.

    •  Names for Reward Tiers: The participant says that the names for the reward tiers might be enhanced because people might not be able to recognize or understand them with the current names. They suggest changing the name to something like "funding rewards".

Marketing

Context of campaign and promotional activities

At Global Gourmet, we curate meal packages from around the globe and bring them straight to your doorstep. We carefully select authentic recipes from different countries, you recreate them using fresh, high-quality ingredients. We include sweet notes from their home country in your own language. Our social media campaign is meant to create awareness about our brand via being a strong emotional brand. To do this make post from countries around the world to make our expat customers feel seen and appreciated.

Reach objective- 800 accounts

 Our reach objective is for our Instagram account to reach a total of 800 accounts by April 14. This will ensure that more people know about our brand and can be possible buyers of our amazing food packages , this can be done in the ‘Professional dashboard’ of a business account under ‘Account insights’

Affect objective

Our marketing affect objective is to create a strong emotional connection with our target audience that goes beyond just promoting our brand. We want to establish ourselves as a trustworthy and reliable source of culturally diverse and authentic cuisine, and build a bond with our customers that is rooted in mutual respect and understanding. This objective is will be measured with the amount of followers our goal is to reach a total of 100 followers by April 14th

Response objective

Seeing that our marketing campaign is all about awareness, our response objective would be for people to click on on our website link. To achieve this goal we will include some text in out post suggesting for people to click on our link and learn more about us for example ‘order now!! link in bio’.

Media Tactics

For our social media campaign we highlight a dish from a different country. One day in the week we will tease the country so our followers can guess the upcoming dish. In the following week we will announce the dish of the week to show appreciation for other cultures. these countries and dishes were chosen based on the countries with the most expats living in the Netherlands based on this statistic provided by Statista(2022). These wore then put into our content calendar for dates to be posted. We also left some space in between to play around with different media types

Target Audience

We aim to target young expats between the ages of 18 and 28 living in the Netherlands. Media Usage According to Statista (2020) Instagram is the 4th most used social media platform in the Netherlands with people between the ages of 20 to 39(Breda University of Applied Sciences - Databanken, n.d.). Statistics by also shows Instagram is the second most checked app after waking up with people between the ages of 18 and 24(Breda University of Applied Sciences - Databanken, n.d.-b). Seeing that our target audience is between the ages of 18 and 28, our target audience fall between both groups tested by Statista and that the research is a few years old we also asked our interviewees in our problem interviews and they stated that Instagram is their most used social media . Relevant habits: Seeing that our target audience falls between both of these According to a survey conducted by Expatica, an online platform for expats, the majority of expats in the Netherlands cook at home regularly. Reflection

One thing that came to our attention is that our attention that our reach objective was easily achieved. we set out to reach a total 800 accounts by the 14th of April and as of today we have surpassed our reach goal by far showing that we have reached 1.3k accounts. We posted a few Insta stories where we go around asking people what meals they miss from home to test out the other forms content. These videos received little to no interactions from our followers. We also experimented with posting the meals together with the teaser of the meals instead of separately and to our surprise this format received more likes that when we posted them separately. We also posted a reel of a customer expressing her joy when she received our package. This format was used to test the reactions and likability we noticed that this post received the most likes as followers may like the personal connection more.

Learning Points

  • Don't be afraid to try new content
  • Be consistent when it comes to posting content
  • Its good to have a basic/consistent plan
  • Design the content in advance so you don't have to do it last minute 
  • Account reaches are easier to achieve than we expected 

Future Planning

Based on the results of our social media campaign, we noticed that our followers responded positively to videos that showcased a personal connection to our brand. As a result, we plan to include more video content in our future posts, such as customer testimonials or behind-the-scenes footage of our team preparing the meals. To ensure that our followers stay engaged and connected with our brand, we will maintain a consistent posting schedule and create content in advance. We also plan to automate our posting to ensure that our content is published at the optimal times for our target audience. Overall, our goal is to establish ourselves as a trustworthy and reliable source of authentic and culturally diverse cuisine. We value diversity, authenticity, and creating a welcoming atmosphere for our customers to explore new cultures through food. Our future plans include expanding our offerings and continuing to engage with our audience on social media to improve our services.

Validation of Assumptions

When it came to making decisions we empathized with our peopled we aimed to target (Expats Living in the Netherlands). We did this by hosting problem interviews with the goal of understanding their struggles to potentially provide them with solutions. We learned that the problems we expected to be important seemed to be less important to them than we thought. For example they have a bigger problem finding traditional food than struggling to find insurance. Decisions like communication channels were chosen by the communication channels were chosen with a combination of research and interviewing our target audience Based on the solution interviews we learned that consumers might actually appreciate a subscription based payment so this feature was included alongside 1 time purchases .
